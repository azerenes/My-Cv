### 🧑‍💼 Professional Summary

I am currently studying **Control and Automation Technology** at **Kırıkkale University** (2024–2026) and graduated from Abidinpaşa Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School in **Electrical Panel Assembly**.

With over 10 years in the electrical field, I worked as an **Electrical Technician at Öz-Er Engineering**, specializing in panel manufacturing, low-current systems, camera installations, cabling, lighting, and fault diagnosis. My internships at Fatih Elektrik and Sondem Elektrik provided extensive field experience.

On the software side, I develop projects using **C**, **Python**, **Kotlin**, and **JavaScript/TypeScript**. My focus areas include embedded systems (ESP32, Arduino), mobile development (Jetpack Compose, React Native), and artificial intelligence (Gemini API). By combining industrial automation experience with software skills, I work at the intersection of **hardware + software + AI**.
